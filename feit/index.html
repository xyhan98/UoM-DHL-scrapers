Dean's Honours List

The Faculty of Engineering and IT acknowledge and congratulate the outstanding achievements of students on the Dean's Honours List.  

The Dean's Honours List celebrates the significant achievements of Engineering and IT students. These students have achieved results that place them in the top 5% of their cohort. Their diligence, dedication and academic excellence are recognised and celebrated each year at the Engineering & IT Awards Night.
