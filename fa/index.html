Dean's Honours List 2023

Our Dean's Honours List recognises the exceptional efforts and abilities of the highest-achieving students in the Faculty of Arts.

Each year's List celebrates students' academic success using a weighted average of credit points across that academic year. The List records:

  • Undergraduate students ranked in the top 3% of their course and year level.
  • Graduate students ranked in the top 3-5% of their course and year level.

Each List is compiled and presented after the end of the relevant academic year.

We hold a celebratory event for members of each year's List and showcase this outstanding achievement as a permanent part of their academic records (recorded on both their Australian Higher Education Graduation Statement and Academic Transcript).

Congratulations to all the current students and recent graduates on the 2023 Dean’s Honours List!

Bachelor of Arts Medal

The Bachelor of Arts Medal recognises the exceptional amount of hard work, commitment and resilience required to achieve and sustain academic excellence throughout an entire degree. As a Faculty, the Medal is one of the deepest measures of pride we can express in a student's achievements.

Students who earn a place in the Dean’s Honours List in their third year are eligible for the Medal, which is then awarded to the eligible student with the highest overall WAM across their entire Bachelor of Arts.

We’re delighted to congratulate two recipients this year! Congratulations to Mr. David Cui and Ms. Nyah Seelig-Schattner as the recipients of the Bachelor of Arts Medal for 2023.
